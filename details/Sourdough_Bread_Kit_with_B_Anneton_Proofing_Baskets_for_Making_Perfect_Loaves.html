Elevate Your Sourdough Bread Game with This Comprehensive Kit

This sourdough bread kit is designed for both novice and experienced bakers, offering all the tools needed to make delicious, artisanal loaves at home. The B Anneton proofing baskets are crafted with premium materials to ensure your sourdough starter remains fresh and active, leading to an exceptional rise and rich flavor in every bake.

The kit includes a 10-inch oval and round proofing basket, ideal for rotating and storing your starter. Paired with a whicker tool and a scraper, it guarantees even breading and a fluffiest texture. Additionally, optional 9-inch round and 4-blade trombones are included for precise shaping and perfect finishing of your loaves.

High-quality materials are at the core of this bread-making kit. It features solid wood, stainless steel, and premium Indonesian white rattan, which not only enhance durability but also contribute to an odor-free and long-lasting baking experience. The beautifully designed packaging and gift box make it an elegant option for gifting to fellow baking enthusiasts.

With 24/7 customer support available to answer any baking-related questions, this kit combines reliability and expert guidance. Whether you're starting your sourdough journey or looking to refine your techniques, this set is built for excellence and is the perfect addition to your kitchen.
